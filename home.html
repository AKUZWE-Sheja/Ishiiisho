COTEMUKI Cooperative is a dynamic cooperative composed entirely of female artisans, showcasing the strength and talent of women entrepreneurs.

Conveniently located along the Kigali-Kayonza Road in Rwamagana, this cooperative has positioned itself near prominent educational institutions, namely Groupe Scolaire St Aloys Rwamagana and Kigali Institute of Education (KIE). This strategic location not only enhances accessibility but also provides a vibrant setting for potential customers.

For inquiries or collaboration opportunities, interested parties can contact COTEMUKI Cooperative at 0783206694.

As a hub of female creativity and entrepreneurship, COTEMUKI Cooperative stands as a testament to the empowering spirit of women in the Rwandan business landscape.
